# SX-002 — Specific symptom timeline documented

**Category:** SX · **Severity:** required · **Pathway group:** `independent`

Applies to CPT 78492, 78452, 93458, 93656.

## Definition
Chart includes specific onset date, progression timeline, frequency, severity, and character of cardiac symptoms.

## Required elements (all three must be present)
- **onset** — Specific onset documented ("X weeks ago", "since March", "over 3 months")
- **frequency** — How often symptoms occur ("daily", "weekly", "with every flight of stairs", "episodic")
- **progression_or_character** — Progression pattern (stable / worsening / improving) OR character (typical / atypical / exertional / rest)

## Validation history

- **2026-04-13** (Peter C5) — Symptoms present but none of onset / frequency / progression documented. System was treating bare symptom mention as satisfying timeline. Drove three-element enforcement.

## Notes

A full symptom timeline requires all three elements. Many clinicians
document onset but not frequency, or frequency but not progression.
Strict enforcement means some cases that look well-documented clinically
still fail SX-002 — that's Peter's explicit intent.
