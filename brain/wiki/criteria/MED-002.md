# MED-002 — Medical therapy duration ≥ 6 weeks documented

**Category:** MED · **Severity:** supporting · **Pathway group:** `independent`

Applies to CPT 93458, 78492.

## Definition
Current medical regimen documented at minimum 6 weeks duration with specific medication names, doses, and start dates.

## Required elements
- **medication_name** — Specific medication name (generic or brand) listed
- **dose** — Specific dose documented (e.g., "metoprolol 50 mg BID")
- **start_date_or_duration** — Start date OR explicit duration ≥ 6 weeks. Presence in a med list WITHOUT duration does NOT satisfy.

## Notes

Common failure mode: chart lists the medication but not when therapy
started. Without duration, MED-002 cannot be met. If the clinician
says "on metoprolol x 8 weeks" we should parse the duration even when
it's not a start_date field.
