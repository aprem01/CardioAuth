# NDX-001 — Non-diagnostic prior stress test documented

**Category:** NDX · **Severity:** required · **Pathway group:** `prior_testing_nondiagnostic`

Applies to CPT 78492, 78491, 78452, 78451, 75557, 75574.

## Definition
Prior stress test (ETT, SPECT, stress echo) was formally classified as non-diagnostic, equivocal, or technically limited with the limitation explicitly stated by the interpreting physician.

## Required elements
- **prior_stress_modality_named** — Specific prior stress modality documented (ETT, SPECT, stress echo, stress MRI).
- **explicit_nondiagnostic_finding** — Interpretation explicitly states "non-diagnostic", "equivocal", "technically limited", "attenuation artifact", "suboptimal", or "false positive". A normal or abnormal result alone does NOT satisfy.

## Pathway group members

- NDX-001 (this criterion) — non-diagnostic stress test
- [NDX-002](NDX-002.md) — Submaximal HR (<85% MPHR)
- [NDX-003](NDX-003.md) — Equivocal ST changes on ETT
- [NDX-004](NDX-004.md) — Technically limited echo

If any one is met, unmet others are **alternative_not_used**.

## Validation history

## Notes

The prior-test-nondiagnostic pathway is the most common justification
for advanced cardiac imaging after an ambiguous initial workup. Peter
emphasized that any ONE of the NDX criteria satisfying means the
pathway is met — the other alternatives shouldn't clutter the gap list.
