# Aetna

## PA rules (cardiology)

### Cardiac PET (CPT 78492)
- Auth required.
- First-pass approval: 74%. Avg decision: 5 days.
- Top denial reasons:
  - Prior stress echo or SPECT result not attached
  - Symptoms documented without timeline (onset/frequency/progression)
  - Clinical indication for PET over SPECT not justified

### Lexiscan SPECT (CPT 78452)
- First-pass approval: 83%. Avg decision: 4.1 days.
- Top denial reasons:
  - Symptoms not sufficiently documented in clinical note
  - No prior noninvasive testing documented

### Stress echo (CPT 93351)
- First-pass approval: 86%.

## Operational gotchas

- **Prior authorization must be obtained BEFORE service date.** Retroactive auth not granted except in documented emergency circumstances.
- **Advanced imaging (PET, SPECT, stress echo) is routed through eviCore** as the utilization management vendor. Submit through eviCore portal, not Aetna directly.

## Submission channels

- eviCore portal for advanced imaging
- Aetna direct for other procedures

## Notes

Aetna's first-pass rate is higher than UHC on 78452 (83% vs 78%) and
on 93351 (86% vs 82%). Worth understanding why — likely looser criteria
or better alignment with ACC AUC scoring. Don't assume our UHC-tuned
taxonomy works 1:1 for Aetna without review.
